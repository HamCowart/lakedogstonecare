Professional Stone Sealing Services

Natural stone is beautiful but vulnerable to moisture, oil, and stains. At LakeDog Stone Care, we apply premium penetrating and enhancing sealers to protect your marble, granite, limestone, slate, and tile. Our Stone Sealing Process

  • Surface Prep: We deep clean to remove oils, residues, and old sealers for a clean bond.
  • Sealer Selection: Based on the stone and usage area, we choose a natural-look or color-enhancing formula.
  • Application: Our team evenly applies the sealer for complete absorption and coverage.
  • Buff & Cure: Excess is wiped away, the surface is buffed, and left to fully cure for lasting protection.

Recommended for new installations, polished surfaces, and after etch or scratch removal. Suitable for kitchen countertops, bathroom vanities, floors, showers, and outdoor stone.
