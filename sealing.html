Professional Stone Sealing Services

Natural stone is beautiful but vulnerable to moisture, oil, and stains. At LakeDog Stone Care, we apply premium penetrating sealers that protect your marble, granite, limestone, slate, and tile without altering their natural appearance. Ideal for kitchens, baths, and showers in the Lake Norman area.

Our Stone Sealing Process

  • Deep Cleaning: We remove grime, soap scum, and residues to ensure proper sealer bonding.
  • Application: Based on the stone type, we apply either a natural look or enhancing sealer.
  • Buffing: The surface is wiped and buffed to eliminate excess sealer and ensure an even coat.
  • Curing: Once cured, your stone will resist stains from oils, water, coffee, wine, and acidic liquids.

This service is essential after polishing or etch removal and recommended for all new or restored stone surfaces.
