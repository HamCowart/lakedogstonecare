Fiberglass Tub & Shower Insert Polishing

Is your fiberglass shower or tub surface looking yellowed, scratched, or cloudy? LakeDog Stone Care offers professional fiberglass tub and shower polishing to restore gloss and clarity to builder-grade units, inserts, and surrounds. Save money by renewing rather than replacing.

Before & After: Fiberglass Refinishing

Our Fiberglass Restoration Process

  • Inspection: We examine the fiberglass for discoloration, residue, and surface damage.
  • Pre-Cleaning: We degrease and clean the surface with a non-acidic, fiberglass-safe cleanser.
  • Polishing: Multi-step buffing and polishing restores gloss and eliminates haze and fine scuffs.
  • Optional Sealant: We can apply a hydrophobic protectant to reduce buildup and make cleaning easier.

This service is ideal for restoring fiberglass shower walls, tub surrounds, or one-piece inserts in bathrooms across Lake Norman.
