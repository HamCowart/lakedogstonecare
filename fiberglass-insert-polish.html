Fiberglass Insert Polishing

Does your fiberglass tub or shower look dull, yellowed, or scratched? We professionally polish fiberglass inserts to remove haze, restore shine, and help protect against future wear. Ideal for builder-grade and pre-fab shower units.

How Fiberglass Polishing Works

  • Surface Evaluation: We check for dullness, scratches, and water stains.
  • Cleaning: Safe, non-damaging cleansers are used to remove buildup and prep the surface.
  • Polishing: A multi-stage buffing and polishing process restores smoothness and brightness.
  • Optional Protection: We can apply a protective coating to resist soap scum and residue buildup.

Perfect for fiberglass tubs, surrounds, shower inserts, and enclosures that need refreshing without replacement.
