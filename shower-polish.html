Stone Shower Cleaning & Polishing Services

Remove soap scum, restore shine, and protect your marble, travertine, limestone, or tile shower. LakeDog Stone Care specializes in polishing and restoring stone showers throughout Mooresville and the Lake Norman area.

Our Shower Polishing & Restoration Process

  • Stone Inspection: We evaluate residue, discoloration, or dullness based on stone type.
  • Safe Cleaning: Non-acidic cleansers remove soap scum and grime without damaging natural stone.
  • Polishing: Abrasive or powder polishing restores smoothness and natural luster.
  • Optional Sealer: Helps protect the stone against water, minerals, and future soap buildup.

Perfect for master showers, custom tile installations, and aging natural stone in need of gentle restoration.
