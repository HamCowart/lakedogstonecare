Stone Shower Cleaning & Polishing Services

Restore the shine and cleanliness of your natural stone shower. At LakeDog Stone Care, we provide professional shower polishing and stone tile restoration for marble, travertine, limestone, and ceramic showers throughout the Lake Norman area.

Our Shower Polishing & Restoration Process

  • Stone Evaluation: We identify grime, discoloration, or mineral buildup based on the stone type.
  • Safe Cleaning: Non-acidic products break down soap scum, residue, and mineral stains without damaging the stone.
  • Polishing: We use abrasives or powders to bring back a clean, smooth, and restored finish.
  • Sealing (Optional): Help your shower stay cleaner longer with a stone-safe sealant that resists future buildup.

This service is perfect for custom marble showers, walk-in stone tile units, or aging tiled bathrooms in need of fresh polish and protection.
