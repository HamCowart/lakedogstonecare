Stone Shower Cleaning & Polishing

Natural stone showers can lose their shine from hard water, soap scum, and daily use. LakeDog Stone Care professionally cleans and polishes marble, travertine, limestone, and tile shower surfaces throughout Mooresville and Lake Norman ‚Äî restoring clarity, luster, and a clean finish.

Our Shower Restoration Process

  • Inspection: We assess the stone type and degree of soap residue, dullness, or hard water etching.
  • Cleaning: We use non-acidic, stone-safe cleansers to dissolve buildup and prepare the surface.
  • Polishing: We polish the stone with abrasives or polishing powders to restore a smooth, uniform finish.
  • Optional Sealing: We can seal the surface to prevent water absorption and future discoloration.

This service is perfect for restoring stone showers in master baths, guest bathrooms, and custom tile enclosures.
