Shower Glass Polishing & Hard Water Stain Removal

Soap scum, hard water stains, and mineral deposits can make your shower doors look dull and neglected. At LakeDog Stone Care, we restore glass clarity using safe, effective techniques to remove buildup and bring your shower back to life.

Before: Cloudy & Stained Shower Glass

After: Clear & Polished Shower Glass

Our Glass Polishing Process

  • Inspection: We evaluate the level of buildup, etching, or staining.
  • Cleaning: We use pH-safe cleansers to break down soap scum and minerals.
  • Polishing: Using non-abrasive pads, we buff away hard water marks and restore a smooth finish.
  • Sealing (Optional): We can apply a water-repellent coating to slow future buildup.
