Expert Etch Mark Removal & Stone Refinishing

Etch marks appear as dull, cloudy patches caused by acidic liquids like lemon juice or vinegar reacting with natural stone. At LakeDog Stone Care, we restore clarity, color consistency, and shine to etched marble, limestone, travertine, and tile surfaces with precise honing and polishing techniques.
